Privacy Policy — Luminify

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Luminify ("the app") is a daily motivational quotes app published by Aatif. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Data We Collect

None. Luminify does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information. The app works entirely offline.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app saves your favorite quotes locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your phone, is not synced to any server, and is removed when you uninstall the app.

Permissions

Luminify does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, storage, or any other sensitive permission. Sharing a quote uses Android's standard share sheet, which you control.

Third-Party Services

Luminify contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics, and no third-party trackers.

Children's Privacy

The app is rated for Everyone and collects no data from users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
