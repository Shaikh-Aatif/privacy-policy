Daily Quotes & Motivation — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Luminify ("the app", "we", "our") handles your information. Luminify is a daily motivational-quotes app published by Aatif. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Short version: Luminify collects no personal data, contains no ads or trackers, and works entirely offline.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Luminify does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email, phone number, or location, and does not track your activity. The app requires no internet connection to function.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

When you mark a quote as a favorite, that preference is saved locally on your device only, using Android's standard SharedPreferences storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to us or any third party
  • Is not synced to any cloud or server
  • Is permanently removed when you uninstall the app

3. Permissions

Luminify does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, photos, files, or any other sensitive device permission. When you share a quote, the app uses Android's built-in share sheet, which you control — Luminify does not access the apps or contacts you share with.

4. Third-Party Services

Luminify contains no advertising networks, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party trackers. No third party receives any information about you through this app.

5. Children's Privacy

Luminify is rated for Everyone and is safe for users of all ages. Because the app collects no data whatsoever, it collects no data from children.

6. Data Security

Since Luminify does not collect or transmit any data, there is no personal data at risk of a breach. Your favorites remain solely on your own device under the protection of your device's operating system.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the revised version will be posted at this same URL with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
